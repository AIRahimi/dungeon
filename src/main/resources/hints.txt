HINT: Attacks against critters deal 100% bonus damage.
HINT: Equipped items are colored purple in your inventory.
HINT: Every combatant movement is considered to be a battle turn.
HINT: If the creature has a weapon, it will be used to perform the attack. Otherwise, the creature will attack with its bare hands.
HINT: The command 'achievements' will also show the total number of achievements.
HINT: There is a 'poem' command that prints a poem to the screen.
